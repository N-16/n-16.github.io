Rainee and the Magical Plant

Program, Design

This is a 2-D platformer game about a girl trying to find the proper planting place for a magic plant. It consists of four levels, the first and the last being for the plot, and the second and third levels are puzzle and parkour level respectively. This game was our two member team's entry for the GDN game jam.

 
Roles Description
Program Programmed for entire game using Unity engine.
Design Designed levels and player mechanics.
Other All the sounds were samples from freesound.org and were slightly edited on fl studio. Skeletal animations.
