Rainee and the Magical Plant

Program, Design

Rainee and the Magical Plant is a platformer game about a girl (played by the player) trying to find the proper planting place for a magic plant. It consists of four levels. The first and the last level are for the plot, and the second and third are puzzle and parkour levels respectively.

This game was our two-member team's entry for the GDN Game Jam 2021.

 
Roles Description
Program Programmed for the entire game using Unity Engine. Notable Sections: Player Movement, Level Mechanics, Dialogue management, UI Management, Sound Management.
Design Designed levels and player mechanics.
Other All the sounds were samples from freesounds.org and were slightly edited on FL Studio, Skeletal animations.
