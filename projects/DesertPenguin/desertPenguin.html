Desert Penguin

Solo Project

Desert Penguin is a 2D endless runner game, in which the player (the penguin) has to dodge bombs and arrow bombs dropped by an airplane while moving forward to avoid dark zone and survive. The airplane may also spawn 'glitches' (Headblock: Spawns a block on random jumps, Fireboots: Reduces health when the player walks over the covered ground, Dig: Random Jumps towards opposite direction) that adds to the chaos of the game.

This game was my submission for the 'HackerEarth's Game Jam 1.0'.

 
Roles Description
Program Programmed for the entire game using Unity engine. Notable Sections: Player Mechanics, Glitch Mechanics, Bomb Spawner/Mechanics, Level Generator.
Other All the sounds were samples from freesound.org and were slightly edited on FL Studio. Design, UI.
