Dots and Boxes

The project, made for AI vs AI battle with a friend, is based on a pen paper game. The game starts with an empty grid of dots. Two players take turns adding a single horizontal or vertical line between two disconnected adjacent dots. The player who completes the fourth side of a box earns one point and takes another turn. The game ends when the grid is full. This game consists of single player mode in which the player plays against the AI.
